# Representative Product Image Notes

This note documents product image files that use a representative product-family photo instead of a
confirmed exact SKU photo.

The goal is to reduce public placeholder visuals while staying honest about product data. These
images should not be treated as confirmation of exact dimensions, material grade, compatible model,
OEM number or final packaging.

## Buyer-Facing Rule

When using representative product-family images, keep the product page wording conservative:

- Do not claim exact length, material grade, current rating or dimensions unless confirmed.
- Do not claim exact compatible brand or OEM number unless confirmed.
- Keep compatibility wording tied to sample, drawing, model number or reference part confirmation.
- Replace representative images with exact product photos when available.
