# Product Image Shooting Guide

Use this guide for ArcFort Weld product images before uploading them to the website.

## Basic Rules

- Use own photos or supplier-authorized images.
- Do not use ecommerce marketplace images without permission.
- Do not remove watermarks from third-party images for commercial website use.
- Do not show brand compatibility, OEM numbers, certifications, current ratings or exact dimensions unless the product data is confirmed.
- Save final images in `public/images/products/`.
- Match the filename in `docs/product-image-checklist.csv` and product data.

## Recommended Photo Style

- Main image: single product on clean white background.
- Optional secondary image: industrial blue background for homepage or category visuals.
- Minimum resolution: 1600 px wide for small parts; 1600 x 1200 px for larger accessories.
- Lighting: soft, even light with no harsh shadow.
- Product angle: show the shape clearly; add close-up shots for thread, orifice, mesh, jaws or connector details.

## Editing Workflow

1. Crop tightly but keep enough whitespace around the product.
2. Remove background or clean it to white.
3. Correct color so copper, ceramic, brass or steel does not look unnatural.
4. Sharpen product edges and important details.
5. Export JPG or WebP for website use.
6. Keep the original photo in a separate source folder if available.
